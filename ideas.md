**Approach A: Institutional Elegance (Navy & Gold)**
- **Design Movement**: British Academic / Institutional Modernism
- **Core Principles**: Authority through restraint, hierarchical clarity, typographic precision, trust through consistency
- **Color Philosophy**: Deep navy (#1a2744) as the dominant institutional color, gold (#c9a84c) as accent for hierarchy markers, warm off-white (#f8f5ef) background to evoke aged paper and tradition

- **Interaction Philosophy**: Deliberate, measured transitions — accordion-style KLA expansion, smooth content fade-in
- **Animation**: 200ms ease-in-out for sidebar selections, 300ms fade for content panel transitions, subtle scale-up on teacher card hover
- **Typography System**: Playfair Display (headings) + Source Sans Pro (body) — classic academic pairing

## Selected Approach: A — Institutional Elegance (Navy & Gold)

This design honours St Stephen's College's heritage and institutional identity. The navy and gold palette directly references the school's colours, while the typographic hierarchy using Playfair Display creates an authoritative, professional directory feel appropriate for an academic staff tool.
